Description:

Multi-colored miniature pipes have long been collectible. The smoking device is not just pleasant to hold in your hands. This is a truly functional tool for those who like to smoke from a straw. Made of heat-resistant borosilicate glass, the mini-tube has sufficient safety margin. Thick, 4mm glass walls ensure impact resistance, no chipping, and no finger burns in a smoking session. And if you cool the mini-pipe well before smoking, then the glass keeps cool for a long time, refreshing and cooling the smoke well.

The use of fuming technology - adding drops of colored glass at the time of manufacture, allows you to make unique and inimitable pipes. A priori, there are no tubes identical in pattern and inclusions. All of them are different, but each is equipped with a fairly roomy chamber where the vegetable mixture is burned. On the side, under the left finger, there is a turbo hole to control the smoking process. The bottom of the smoking bowl is flat so that the pipe stands flat on the surface without rolling or tipping over.
