Our delivery is free and takes 7 to 21 days to arrive.

What are the criteria for a refund?
1. Damaged product
2. The product has not arrived or does not arrive for more than 30 days
You can get a 10% discount if the delivery arrives after 21 days
If you have a problem that is not among the points above, you can solve this problem by sending a message with your problem to us on Instagram
